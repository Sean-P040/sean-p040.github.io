A Brief Introduction About Me

My Story

Greetings, thank you for visiting my personal website! I am a tech enthusiast born and raised in the beautiful county of St. Louis, MO. Growing up amidst the transition into a new millennium, deemed as the "Information Age" by many, I quickly gained interest in the latest and greatest technological inventions. During the initial stages of my academic career, I gravitated towards specializing in business administration. However, it was not long before I became aware of the unique Information Systems & Technology Bachelor's degree program, offered at UMSL. The IS program offers the best of both worlds in terms of my interests; A foundation in both the functional areas of business and in computing technologies. I immediately enrolled into this program in the Fall of 2019.

Fast forward two years– I just recently graduated this Spring. I would describe myself as a person possessing an inquisitive and analytical mind, with a burning desire to acquire new knowledge. My specialty is in utilizing computer systems and innovative technical solutions to solve various business challenges. Right now, I am seeking an opportunity that will provide a professional experience, in which I can showcase these skills in a real-world setting.

My Interests

I love learning new things, especially related to technology! When I am not working on a project or learning a new language, I will most likely be outdoors enjoying nature and/or exercising. One of my long-term goals has been to acquire the knowledge and skills appropriate to provide others with beneficial services. As my capabilities and level of understanding of various topics progresses, I realize I am stepping closer to achieving this goal.
