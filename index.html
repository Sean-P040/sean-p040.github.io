My Story

Greetings, thank you for visiting my personal website! I am a tech enthusiast born and raised in the beautiful county of St. Louis, MO. Growing up amidst the transition into a new millennium, deemed as the "Information Age" by many, I quickly gained interest in the latest and greatest technological inventions. During the initial stages of my academic career, I gravitated towards specializing in business administration. However, it was not long before I became aware of the unique Information Systems & Technology Bachelor's degree program, offered at UMSL. The IS program offers the best of both worlds in terms of my interests; A foundation in both the functional areas of business and in computing technologies. I immediately enrolled into this program in the Fall of 2019.

Fast forward two years– I currently have just one semester remaining until graduating. I would describe myself as a person possessing an inquisitive and analytical mind, with a burning desire to acquire new knowledge. My specialty is in utilizing computer systems and innovative technical solutions to solve various business challenges. Right now, I am seeking an opportunity that will provide a professional experience, in which I can showcase these skills in a real-world setting.
